🎰Roll 'n Risk!🎮

Roll 'n Risk is a thrilling two-player dice game that blends strategy with chance!

  • Players take turns to roll a 🎲 dice as many times as they want during their turn
  • Each dice roll adds its value to the player’s current score 🎯
  • If a player rolls a 1 their current score is lost and it becomes the next player’s turn.

  • To avoid losing points, a player can click the “Hold”📥 button to save their current score into their total score
  • The first player to reach the target score (e.g., 100 points) wins the game!

    • Tip: Take calculated risks — roll wisely, and know when to hold!
